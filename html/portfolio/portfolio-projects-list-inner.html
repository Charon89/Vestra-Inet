Lock 8

Key Features

  • Custom ERP with rental functionality
  • Inventory management system
  • Order management system
  • Custom work order and service order creation tools
  • Intelligent order form and quotation generation
  • Long-term and short-term rental support
  • Accounting system
  • Custom reporting functionality

About Lock8

Lock 8 provides you with the best in brand name for a competitive price, all at your convenience. We also design and manufacture our own line of welding rack systems for multiple job site uses. From manufacturing to mining and everything in between Lock 8 Equipment offers the knowledge and experience necessary to ensure project success when it comes to having the best service, equipment, and consumables necessary – regardless of size and scope of project.

Type of Systems Used in Project

Custom ERP with rental functionality

The custom ERP solution for Lock8 offers specialized functionality for tracking orders, managing inventory, and providing options for high-level reporting. Uniquely, the ERP also includes a rental management component to offer products for rent or lease instead of purchase. The software is used to assign prices, create recurring rentals, assign statuses to items, and to track the life cycle of a rented item from the moment it is made available to the moment it is returned.

Order management system

Customers can place orders for consumable inventory items or rental items using the website, email, or even telephone. Orders are converted into quotes, which are mailed to customers for approval before being converted to work orders. Work orders automatically change inventory levels to eliminate stock inconsistencies.

Maintenance and service tracking

Returned rental items can be assigned custom statuses, such as Maintenance or Repair. Internal work orders can be created to calculate the cost of maintenance and sent to different departments for processing.

Custom accounting system

The accounting system for Lock8 was upgraded with custom reporting and key functionality such as accounts receivable, accounts payable, credit balance information, and general ledger information to ensure accuracy and precision in daily operations.

Lock8 supplies high-quality welding equipment for contractors and skilled tradespeople. They sell new and refurbished tooling as well as gases. Gas cylinders and specialty equipment can also be rented instead of being purchased.


A full ERP solution was required to manage the entire inventory of both consumable items and rental equipment. This software needed to manage orders, stock levels, and internal work orders as well as providing a method to accurately track rental equipment to avoid incurring expensive losses through human error. Additionally, the accounting system required an upgrade to meet the unique business requirements of Lock8.
