Social Media Marketing or Time To Get Social

For local businesses in Toronto, Facebook, Twitter, and Google+ pages have become an increasingly important part of the SEO process. It’s an easy way to interact with potential customers and keeping your business fresh so that Google recognizes your prolific activity.


Social media also enhances your online presence by making your company accessible through non-Google searches. If a user enters a relevant product name in a Facebook search for example, your company’s page will appear among the results.
